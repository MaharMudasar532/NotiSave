MiningPower Privacy Policy

MiningPower collects only the information needed to authenticate users, record mining activity, upload audio recordings, and back up gallery images when a user enables backup.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, password, referral code, and bank details.
  • Mining activity data including balances, referrals, withdrawals, and storage usage.
  • Audio files created by the app while foreground audio recording is enabled.
  • Gallery images only when the backup setting is enabled and photo access is granted.
  • Basic technical data needed to deliver the service securely.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To operate mining, referral, withdrawal, and admin features.
  • To upload and store user audio recordings and backed-up gallery images.
  • To protect accounts, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

Permissions

  • Microphone permission is used only for foreground audio recording.
  • Photo or storage permission is used only to upload gallery images when backup is turned on.
  • Internet access is used to connect the app to the MiningPower backend.

Data Sharing

MiningPower does not sell personal data. Data is shared only with infrastructure and service providers needed to host, secure, and operate the application.

Data Retention and Security

We keep data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect stored data.

User Choices

  • You can disable gallery backup from the app settings.
  • You can revoke microphone or photo permissions from system settings at any time.
  • You can contact us to request account updates or deletion, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the MiningPower team at support@miningpower.app.
